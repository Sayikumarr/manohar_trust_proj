OUR MISSION

  • To create a Society where every Child is equipped with food, shelter, education and good health which is equipped with strong human relationships and safe environment.
  • To Conduct Continuous food donations programs through tieups with various Orphanages etc.,
  • To provide educational/Medical aid to underprivileged People.
  • To conduct Marathons/Seminars for creating awareness on multiple social issues.

OBJECTIVES

  • Development of Human Resources by spreading Quality Education.
  • Creating Health Awareness among the youth by conducting Health Camps.
  • The Trust acts as a bridge between needy and philanthropists and donors.
  • The Trust strives to spread moral values among the youth.
  • Tries to create environmental awareness in the youth.
  • Motivates the students for better future.
  • To encourage, inculcate and promote self-confidence and discipline among people for their career development for providing vocational training to generate self-employment.
  • To award scholarships, stipends and distribution of books to such bright students coming from poor and disadvantaged families, from time to time as shall be decided by the trustees.
  • To undertake, promote and coordinate programs of cultural, spiritual, leadership development, patriotism and national integration.
